quiz:
  - q: "What is perimeter?"
    a: "The total distance around the outside of a 2D shape."
  - q: "What is the formula for the perimeter of a rectangle?"
    a: "P = 2(l + w)"
  - q: "What is the formula for the circumference of a circle?"
    a: "C = 2πr or C = πd"
  - q: "What is area?"
    a: "The amount of space inside a 2D shape. Measured in squared units (cm², m²)."
  - q: "What is the formula for the area of a triangle?"
    a: "A = ½ × base × perpendicular height"
  - q: "Why must you use the PERPENDICULAR height, not the slant side?"
    a: "The perpendicular height is the true vertical distance from base to top (at 90°). The slant side is longer and gives the wrong answer."
  - q: "What is surface area?"
    a: "The total area of ALL the faces of a 3D shape. Imagine unfolding it into a flat net."
  - q: "What is the formula for the surface area of a rectangular prism?"
    a: "SA = 2(lw + lh + wh)"
  - q: "What is volume?"
    a: "The amount of space inside a 3D shape. Measured in cubed units (cm³, m³)."
  - q: "What is the formula for the volume of a triangular prism?"
    a: "V = area of triangle × length = (½ × b × h) × l"
  - q: "What is the golden rule for capacity?"
    a: "1 cm³ = 1 mL (exactly)"
  - q: "Convert 1 m³ to litres."
    a: "1 m³ = 1,000 L"
  - q: "A fish tank is 50cm × 30cm × 40cm. How many litres does it hold?"
    a: "V = 60,000 cm³ = 60,000 mL = 60 L"
  - q: "A cube has side length 6 cm. Find its volume and surface area."
    a: "Volume = 6³ = 216 cm³. Surface area = 6 × 6² = 216 cm²."

glossary:
  - topic: "Perimeter"
    terms:
      - term: "Perimeter"
        emoji: "📏"
        definition: "The total distance around the outside of a 2D shape. Measured in cm, m, or km."
      - term: "Circumference"
        emoji: "⭕"
        definition: "The perimeter of a circle. Formula: C = 2πr or C = πd."
      - term: "Radius"
        emoji: "📐"
        definition: "The distance from the centre of a circle to its edge. Half of the diameter."
      - term: "Diameter"
        emoji: "↔️"
        definition: "The distance across a circle through its centre. Double the radius (d = 2r)."
      - term: "Pi (π)"
        emoji: "🥧"
        definition: "A special number ≈ 3.14. The ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter. It never ends!"
  - topic: "Area"
    terms:
      - term: "Area"
        emoji: "⬜"
        definition: "The amount of space inside a 2D shape. Measured in squared units (cm², m²)."
      - term: "Perpendicular Height"
        emoji: "📐"
        definition: "The vertical distance from the base to the top of a shape. Must be at 90° to the base — NOT the slant side!"
      - term: "Composite Shape"
        emoji: "🧩"
        definition: "A shape made of two or more simpler shapes joined together. Find the area of each part, then add or subtract."
      - term: "Base"
        emoji: "➖"
        definition: "The bottom side of a shape that you measure height from."
  - topic: "Surface Area & Volume"
    terms:
      - term: "Surface Area"
        emoji: "📦"
        definition: "The total area of ALL the faces of a 3D shape. Imagine unfolding it into a flat net. Measured in cm² or m²."
      - term: "Volume"
        emoji: "🧊"
        definition: "The amount of space inside a 3D shape. Measured in cubed units (cm³, m³)."
      - term: "Cross-Section"
        emoji: "🔪"
        definition: "The 2D shape you see when you slice through a 3D shape. For a prism, it's the same all the way through."
      - term: "Prism"
        emoji: "📐"
        definition: "A 3D shape with the same cross-section along its whole length. Examples: rectangular prism, triangular prism."
      - term: "Net"
        emoji: "📋"
        definition: "A flat pattern that can be folded up to make a 3D shape."
  - topic: "Capacity"
    terms:
      - term: "Capacity"
        emoji: "🥛"
        definition: "The amount of liquid a container can hold. Measured in millilitres (mL) or litres (L)."
      - term: "1 cm³ = 1 mL"
        emoji: "🔑"
        definition: "The golden rule! One cubic centimetre holds exactly one millilitre of liquid."
      - term: "1 L = 1,000 mL"
        emoji: "🔄"
        definition: "One litre equals one thousand millilitres. To convert L → mL, multiply by 1,000."

quiz:
  - q: "What is perimeter?"
    a: "The total distance around the outside of a 2D shape."
  - q: "What is the formula for the perimeter of a rectangle?"
    a: "P = 2(l + w)"
  - q: "What is the formula for the circumference of a circle?"
    a: "C = 2πr or C = πd"
  - q: "What is area?"
    a: "The amount of space inside a 2D shape. Measured in squared units (cm², m²)."
  - q: "What is the formula for the area of a triangle?"
    a: "A = ½ × base × perpendicular height"
  - q: "Why must you use the PERPENDICULAR height, not the slant side?"
    a: "The perpendicular height is the true vertical distance from base to top (at 90°). The slant side is longer and gives the wrong answer."
  - q: "What is surface area?"
    a: "The total area of ALL the faces of a 3D shape. Imagine unfolding it into a flat net."
  - q: "What is the formula for the surface area of a rectangular prism?"
    a: "SA = 2(lw + lh + wh)"
  - q: "What is volume?"
    a: "The amount of space inside a 3D shape. Measured in cubed units (cm³, m³)."
  - q: "What is the formula for the volume of a triangular prism?"
    a: "V = area of triangle × length = (½ × b × h) × l"
  - q: "What is the golden rule for capacity?"
    a: "1 cm³ = 1 mL (exactly)"
  - q: "Convert 1 m³ to litres."
    a: "1 m³ = 1,000 L"
  - q: "A fish tank is 50cm × 30cm × 40cm. How many litres does it hold?"
    a: "V = 60,000 cm³ = 60,000 mL = 60 L"
  - q: "A cube has side length 6 cm. Find its volume and surface area."
    a: "Volume = 6³ = 216 cm³. Surface area = 6 × 6² = 216 cm²."
